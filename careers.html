Join us

Sloths Intel is a small, high-trust team based in Cambridge that works openly with talent from Cambridgeshire, London, and Hong Kong.

Data Science Intern (FT/PT)

You will support modelling designs and hybrid intelligence work, including data preparation, evaluation, and small prototypes testing. Roles are paid and based in Cambridgeshire or London.

Data Scientist (FT/PT)

You will design predictive models, evaluate hybrid intelligence systems, and present visualisations. Roles are paid and based in Cambridgeshire or London.

Research Collaboration (Open applications)

If you enjoy turning ambiguity into usable tools — calm interfaces, reliable logic, and decision-ready outputs — we’re open to a conversation even without a fixed role.

How to apply

Please fill out the contact form or email us with a short intro, your availability (internship / part-time / full-time), and links to your work (GitHub, portfolio, papers, or case studies). If possible, include one example of a problem you solved and how you approached it.

Sloths Intel is an equal opportunity organisation and welcomes applicants regardless of background.

Team

We work as a focused core team and collaborate with specialists when needed. Our default is deliberate execution: clarity first, then delivery.
