About

Sloths are often mistaken for slow.

In reality, they are efficient and deliberate — evolved to operate in complex environments with minimal waste.

That philosophy guides Sloths Intel. We design AI systems that ignore noise, conserve effort, and act only when it matters.

The result is intelligence that stays clear under pressure — reliable and easier to trust when decisions count.

Approach

Real-world organisations work with imperfect data and limited resources.

We use hybrid intelligence — combining raw data, machine learning, domain knowledge, and human judgment, to defend your decisions.

This produces practical systems that support confident decisions without black boxes.

Pricing

We price work based on scope, data complexity, and the decision you need to make.

Our default is small, time-boxed work first — then scale when it’s proving value.
