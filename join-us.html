Internships: AI / Data Science Intern (paid)

Support applied modelling and decision support work, including data preparation, evaluation, and small prototypes tested with real users. Roles are paid and based in Cambridgeshire or London.

Open applications: Engineering / Research Collaboration

If you enjoy turning ambiguity into usable tools — calm interfaces, reliable logic, and decision-ready outputs — we’re open to a conversation even without a fixed role.

How to apply

Email recruitment@slothsintel.com with a short intro, your availability (internship / part-time / full-time), and links to your work (GitHub, portfolio, papers, or case studies). If possible, include one example of a problem you solved and how you approached it.

Sloths Intel is an equal opportunity organisation and welcomes applicants regardless of background.
