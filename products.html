Products Hub

The products listed below are representative showcases and can be tailored or extended to meet specific client needs.

Products suite

AutoTrac

AutoTrac is a portable application that allows freelancers and small teams understand where their time and income actually go.

It combines simple tracking, lightweight analytics, and clear summaries without forcing rigid workflows.

The metadata is exportable for better visibility, fairer pricing, and more confident planning.

AutoWeave

AutoWeave is a web application that helps small businesses bring scattered metadata together and get it ready for analysis.

It automates data collection, cleaning, and organisation across spreadsheets, tools, and services.

The result is clean, usable data without the usual manual effort for downstream processing.

AutoPred

AutoPred (coming soon!) is a web application that enables individuals and small teams make better sense of their wrapped data and plan ahead with confidence.

It supports forecasting, simple scenario comparisons, and early signals without requiring complex setups.

The result is clearer expectations, better decisions, and fewer surprises.

AutoVisuals

AutoVisuals is a pipeline that helps creators and teams produce consistent, high-quality visual assets at scale.

It streamlines prompt-to-asset workflows, metadata management, and quality control, reducing manual effort.

The result is faster production with fewer rejections and a cleaner, repeatable pipeline.
